What Is Brainitz?

SAVE TIME

Simply add questions throughout a chosen video once. Use the video lesson in class, for homework for absent students, for review.

STUDENT ACHIEVEMENT

Questions pop up throughout the video lesson. If an answer is incorrect the video backs up to reteach. Short answer essays are evaluated in real time

COLLABORATE

Evaluate the student data. Share your lessons, use the lessons from other teachers.
